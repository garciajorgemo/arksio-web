Features

Everything your family
needs in one place.

Built for families with multiple nationalities, frequent travelers, and anyone who has ever missed a renewal deadline.

Family Profiles
Add unlimited family members — each with their own documents. See everyone's expiry dates in one dashboard, sorted by urgency.
Smart Reminders
Configurable alerts at 12 months, 6 months, 3 months, 1 month, and 1 week before expiry. Never be caught off guard again.
AI Renewal Guide
Tap any document to get a personalized, location-aware renewal guide powered by Claude AI. Step-by-step instructions for your specific situation.
Calendar Integration
Export any expiry date directly to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook with a single tap. Stays in sync automatically.
Secure Document Sharing
Generate a PDF of any document and share it via WhatsApp, email, or iMessage. You choose exactly what to include — name, dates, photo, document number.
All Document Types
Passports, visas, driver's licenses, Global Entry, residency permits, insurance cards, professional licenses, and more — all in one place.

Privacy

Your data stays
on your device.

We built Arksio with a simple principle: your sensitive documents should never leave your control. Period.

Local storage only
All data is stored on your iPhone and backed up to your personal iCloud. Arksio has zero access to your files.
Biometric lock
Face ID and Touch ID protect access to your documents every time you open the app.
No tracking, no ads
We don't use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools. We don't know how you use the app.
We never sell your data
Garmor Ventures LLC does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Ever.

Zero knowledge architecture

Your documents, photos, and sensitive data never touch our servers. What's on your phone stays on your phone.
